# Monthly Investment Report

description: Generates a risk-weighted investment recommendation for a $50/month budget across USA, India, and Crypto (Bitcoin). Triggers on "generate monthly report", "monthly advice", or "picks for [Month]".

## Workflow

### Step 1: Sector & Country Deep-Dive
You will receive **live market data** (prices, 52-week ranges, news) for a curated watchlist. Use this real data — do not invent prices or news.
Research the current state of the hardwired sectors in USA and India:
*   Semiconductors/Hardware (USA/India)
*   Space Industry (USA/India)
*   Green Energy (No fossil fuels)
*   Defense
*   Health/Biotech
*   Bitcoin (BTC)

### Step 2: Risk-Weighted Selection
Select three candidates based on the 60/25/15 allocation. **All picks must be NYSE or NASDAQ listed** (Robinhood-tradeable). Use ADRs/ETFs for India exposure.

| Tier | Weight | Risk Level | Target Sectors |
|------|--------|------------|----------------|
| **Anchor** | 60% ($30) | Low-Med | Defense, Semiconductor Leaders, Healthcare |
| **Growth** | 25% ($12.50) | Med-High | Renewable Energy, Bitcoin ETFs/equities, India ADRs/ETFs |
| **Moonshot** | 15% ($7.50) | Very High | Space, Early Biotech, Crypto-adjacent equities |

### Step 3: Analysis (Institutional Quality)
For each pick, identify:
1.  **Investment Thesis**: Why this, why now?
2.  **Specific Catalyst**: What event will drive value?
3.  **Key Risk**: The #1 reason this could fail.

### Step 4: Format Email

#### Example Format:
---
**Subject: Your $50 Roadmap for [Month] [Year] (USA, India & Bitcoin)**

**1. The Anchor ($30) - [Ticker/Name]**
*   **Risk:** Low-Medium | **Sector:** [Sector]
*   **Thesis:** [1-2 sentences]

**2. The Growth Driver ($12.50) - [Ticker/Name]**
*   **Risk:** Medium-High | **Sector:** [Sector]
*   **Thesis:** [1-2 sentences]

**3. The Moonshot ($7.50) - [Ticker/Name]**
*   **Risk:** Very High | **Sector:** [Sector]
*   **Thesis:** [1-2 sentences]

**Note:** This plan fully allocates your $50 budget via fractional shares on Robinhood. Dollar amounts, not share counts, drive each allocation.
---

## Guidelines
*   **Strictly No Fossil Fuels.**
*   Focus on USA and India only.
*   **All picks MUST trade on NYSE or NASDAQ** — no OTC, Pink Sheet, or foreign-only listings.
*   India exposure via NYSE/NASDAQ-listed ADRs (INFY, WIT, HDB, AZRE, etc.) or US-domiciled India ETFs (INDA, SMIN, PIN) only.
*   Bitcoin/Crypto exposure via NYSE/NASDAQ-listed instruments only (ETFs: IBIT, FBTC; equities: COIN, MSTR) — never direct crypto exchange tickers.
*   Before outputting any ticker, verify it is NYSE or NASDAQ listed.
*   **Fractional shares are supported.** Always frame allocations in dollar amounts ($30 / $12.50 / $7.50), never in share counts. Any price-per-share is valid.
*   Use the live market data provided — do not fabricate prices or news.
