# Monthly Investment Report

description: Generates a risk-weighted investment recommendation for a $50/month budget across USA and India markets. Triggers on "generate monthly report", "monthly advice", or "picks for [Month]".

## Workflow

### Step 1: Sector & Country Deep-Dive
Research the current state of the hardwired sectors in USA and India:
*   Semiconductors/Hardware (USA/India)
*   Space Industry (USA/India)
*   Green Energy (No fossil fuels)
*   Defense
*   Health/Biotech

### Step 2: Risk-Weighted Selection
Select three candidates based on the 60/25/15 allocation:

| Tier | Weight | Risk Level | Target Sectors |
|------|--------|------------|----------------|
| **Anchor** | 60% ($30) | Low-Med | Defense, Semiconductor Leaders, Healthcare |
| **Growth** | 25% ($12.50) | Med-High | Renewable Energy, IT Services, Emerging India |
| **Moonshot** | 15% ($7.50) | Very High | Space, Early Biotech, Nano-cap Semiconductors |

### Step 3: Analysis (Institutional Quality)
For each pick, identify:
1.  **Investment Thesis**: Why this, why now? (e.g., "India's semiconductor mission is accelerating").
2.  **Specific Catalyst**: What event will drive value?
3.  **Key Risk**: The #1 reason this could fail.

### Step 4: Format Email

#### Example Format:
---
**Subject: Your $50 Roadmap for [Month] [Year] (USA & India Focus)**

**1. The Anchor ($30) - [Ticker/Name]**
*   **Risk:** Low-Medium | **Sector:** [Sector]
*   **Thesis:** [1-2 sentences]
*   **Catalyst:** [Specific event]

**2. The Growth Driver ($12.50) - [Ticker/Name]**
*   **Risk:** Medium-High | **Sector:** [Sector]
*   **Thesis:** [1-2 sentences]

**3. The Moonshot ($7.50) - [Ticker/Name]**
*   **Risk:** Very High | **Sector:** [Sector]
*   **Thesis:** [1-2 sentences]

**Note:** This plan fully allocates your $50 budget.
---

## Guidelines
*   **Strictly No Fossil Fuels.**
*   Focus on USA and India only.
*   Prioritize ETFs for the Anchor tier.
