# Monthly Investment Report

description: Generates a simple, three-point investment recommendation for a $50/month budget. Triggers on "generate monthly report", "where should I put my $50", "this month's picks", or "monthly investment advice".

## Workflow

### Step 1: Sector Review
Check the current performance and news for the 5 hardwired pillars:
*   Total US Market
*   Information Technology
*   Healthcare
*   Energy/Sustainability
*   International

### Step 2: Identify "Top 3"
Select three candidates. For each, identify:
1.  **Ticker Symbol** (e.g., VOO)
2.  **Current Price** (Note if fractional shares are needed)
3.  **Expense Ratio** (Must be low)
4.  **Key Reason for Selection** (e.g., "Tech is rebounding", "Energy prices are low")

### Step 3: Format Email
Draft the recommendation in a clean, email-ready format.

#### Example Format:
---
**Subject: Your $50 Roadmap for [Month] [Year]**

Hello! Here is your simplified investment plan for this month. 

**1. The Anchor: [Ticker] ([Name])**
*   **Sector:** [Pillar Name]
*   **Why:** [Simple explanation]
*   **Suggested Amount:** $25

**2. The Growth Opportunity: [Ticker] ([Name])**
*   **Sector:** [Pillar Name]
*   **Why:** [Simple explanation]
*   **Suggested Amount:** $15

**3. The Future Focus: [Ticker] ([Name])**
*   **Sector:** [Pillar Name]
*   **Why:** [Simple explanation]
*   **Suggested Amount:** $10

---

## Guidelines
*   Keep descriptions under 2 sentences.
*   Focus on "Low Cost" and "Long Term".
*   Always mention the total budget is $50.
